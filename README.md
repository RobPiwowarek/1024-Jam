# 1024-Jam
Project for GameDev Science Club "Polygon"'s "1024 Jam"
or at least it was supposed to be. But turns out university can be hard at times. 

Current idea for this game is that as you fly your spaceship you encounter 10 types of enemies tagged 2^k where k = {0, 1, .., 9} + boss enemies. You have 10 types of bullets (Numpad 0-9) and only bullet 2 can destroy enemy tagged 2^1 etc. 

Trying to keep the theme, we will iterate and (probably/hopefully) make it somewhat interesting to play.
